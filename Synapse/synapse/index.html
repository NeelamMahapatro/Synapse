PSI-PHI

Hackathon By AXIOM, NIT ROURKELA

Greetings!


Axiom, the official Mathematics club of Nit Rourkela is completing it's decade long ruling in the hearts of NIT Rourkela. Beholding this moment, we announce with pleasure that we shall organise a mathematics fest SYNAPSE in January 2019. This fest shall cover the interests of school and college students alike and is aimed at solving real world problems, helping provide a better life to people. It is definitely going to be a complete package, full of fun and entertainment. This event-packed fest will constitute of a hackathon Psi-phi, a school level Olympiad named MTSE, guest talks by some renowned personalities in this very field. Psi-phi, the hackathon will be a 36 hour online event where participants all over the country will be taking part and showcase their skills and excel as well. MTSE will be a olympaid where students from classes 6 to 12 will be participating. The distinguished performers will be awarded in every case. Wishing forward to an ample participation from each one of you in this amazing fest. Cheers.
